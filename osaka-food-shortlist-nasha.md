## ⭐ Must-Go Shortlist
### Sukiyaki Tsuruya / 鶴や (IG Reel)
- **Why go:** Restaurant / Sukiyaki & Shabu-shabu
- **Must-try:** Wagyu & Kobe beef sukiyaki with raw egg dipping
- **Budget:** ¥5,000 (~฿1,100)
- **Access:** From Shinsaibashi Station: ~10 min
- **Photos:** 2 images saved

### Kaiten Sushi Jin / 回転寿司 仁 (IG Reel)
- **Why go:** Restaurant / Sushi
- **Must-try:** Fatty tuna (maguro) + big shrimp
- **Budget:** From ~¥100/plate (~฿22+)
- **Access:** From Shinsaibashi Station: ~15–20 min
- **Photos:** 3 images saved

### Yakiniku Gorichan Shinsaibashi / 焼肉ごりちゃん (IG Reel)
- **Why go:** Restaurant / BBQ
- **Must-try:** 50mm thick-cut beef tongue (タン元) - rare tongue root part
- **Budget:** Lunch ~¥3,000+
- **Access:** From Shinsaibashi Station: ~3 min
- **Photos:** 2 images saved

### Nikai no Oasis KITTE Osaka / ニカイノオアシス KITTE大阪
- **Why go:** Puri-puri shrimp cutlet, grilled giant river prawns, casual tempura izakaya vibe
- **Must-try:** Puri-puri ebi katsu (shrimp cutlet), grilled prawns
- **Budget:** Shrimp cutlet ¥780 (~฿187)
- **Location:** 大阪府大阪市北区梅田3-2-2 KITTE大阪 B1F
- **Access:** From LEOPARD Shinsaibashi: 🚇 Midosuji Line (Shinsaibashi → Umeda) ~8 min + 🚶 ~6 min walk to KITTE Osaka B1F (west gate side). Taxi ~12–18 min depending traffic.
- **Map:** https://maps.google.com/?q=大阪府大阪市北区梅田3-2-2+KITTE大阪+B1F
- **Photos:** 3 images saved

### Sushi Kou / すし幸 (Tsuruhashi)
- **Why go:** Standing sushi; tuna and eel-heavy counter style
- **Budget:** Budget to mid (reel highlight: eel/anago ~¥250 per piece)
- **Location:** 大阪府大阪市天王寺区舟橋町20-13
- **Access:** ~110m from JR Tsuruhashi Station
- **Map:** https://maps.google.com/?q=大阪府大阪市天王寺区舟橋町20-13

### Uoshin Sakaba Umeda Teppan / 魚心酒場 梅田てっぺん部 (FB Reel)
- **Why go:** All-you-can-eat grilled oysters + chutoro option
- **Must-try:** Grilled oysters + chutoro
- **Budget:** Oyster AYCE 90 min ¥4,980 incl. tax; Chutoro AYCE add-on +¥1,000 (promo conditions may apply)
- **Location:** 4-16 Doyamacho, Kita-ku, Osaka
- **Access:** From Umeda Station: ~5 min walk
- **Map:** https://maps.google.com/?q=Uoshin+Sakaba+Umeda+Teppan+4-16+Doyamacho+Osaka
- **Photos:** 3 images saved
- **Status:** 🆕 newly added
