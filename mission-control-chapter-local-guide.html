How to think about this area

This part of Bangkok works best when you use it as a layered neighborhood. You have immediate convenience, better food than most people realize, easy access to Samyan Mitrtown, and quick jumps to Bang Rak, Yaowarat, Banthat Thong, Silom, and Sathorn.

It is not only about being near one landmark. It is strong because it gives you a practical base with enough texture to actually enjoy living here.
