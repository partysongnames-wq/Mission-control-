JR Sanyo–San’in Area Pass (7-day)

≈ 4,638 THB per person

Valid seven consecutive days; perfect for the Kansai → Hiroshima → Fukuoka corridor.

Coverage & inclusions
  • Sanyo Shinkansen (Shin-Osaka ↔ Hiroshima ↔ Fukuoka)
  • JR Kansai + San’in regional lines
  • JR ferry to Miyajima
Example travel times
  • Osaka → Hiroshima: ~1h30
  • Hiroshima → Fukuoka: ~1h00
  • Osaka → Fukuoka: ~2h30
